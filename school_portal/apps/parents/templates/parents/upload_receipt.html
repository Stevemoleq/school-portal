How this works

  1. Visit any bank branch and deposit the school fees to the official school account.
  2. Collect the stamped bank deposit slip.
  3. Fill in the form below and upload a clear photo/scan of the slip.
  4. The school accountant will verify and credit your account — usually within 24 hours.
